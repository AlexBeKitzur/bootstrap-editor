I want this editor

Simple, versatile, remarkable, and free.

In my humble opinion, the folks at Medium got it right: their editor is everything necessary to expression through words. A mashup of CKEditor, Bootstrap, and Font Awesome, this library is my homage to their combined blood, sweat, and tears.

Suprise! This document is editable. This is my editor:

  • Inline editing, built on CKEditor
  • Custom, simplified toolbar that appears when text is selected

This is a work in progress.
