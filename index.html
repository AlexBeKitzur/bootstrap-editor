bootstrap-editor

Simple, versatile, remarkable, and free.

In my humble opinion, the folks at Medium got it right: their editor is everything necessary to expression through words. A mashup of CKEditor, Bootstrap, and Font Awesome, this library is my homage to their combined blood, sweat, and tears.

This page is editable, and this is my editor:

  • Inline, distraction free editing built on CKEditor
  • Minimalistic toolbar that appears only when text is selected
  • A simplified, pluggable event model

This is a work in progress. You can contribute by working on the issues.
